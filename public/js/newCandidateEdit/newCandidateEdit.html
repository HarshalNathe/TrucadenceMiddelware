ATTENDANCE POLICY

Punctuality and reliable attendance are important to your success on any assignment. If you must be absent or late because of an illness or an emergency, contact your Trucadence office at (630) 394-4707 prior to your shift start time. If necessary, leave a voice message including your name and assignment location.  Trucadence has a strict attendance policy for all assignments.  If you have one (1) unapproved attendance incident in the first 30-day period, your assignment will be ended. This includes any and all tardiness, leaving early and/or unapproved absences.  If you know in advance that you will need time off, please contact Trucadence immediately to make the necessary arrangements.

Additionally, I agree to the following company policies:

  1. Adhere to all company policies, including but not limited to safety, while on assignment
  2. Follow the working schedule and complete the assigned shifts/hours
  3. Communication
    • In the event the assignment is ended, I will contact Trucadence, twice per week, for other available assignments
    • Contact us if you cannot begin the assignment, arrive on time, or complete the job
    • Contact us if you are injured while on the job
    • Contact us if you require assistance while on the job
    • Contact us if the client asks you to return or offers you a full-time position
    • Contact us if a client asks you to work overtime
    • Contact us if you change your name, phone number, or address
